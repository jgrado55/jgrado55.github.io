What is there to see in the Villa Chamber?

The Villa Chamber offers a warm and immersive retreat inspired by The Mexican Revolution. Earthy tones, lantern-style lighting, and handcrafted Wooden furnishings create a nostalgic hacienda ambiance, while portraits of Pancho Villa and Emiliano Zapata add a touch of historic character.

A carved wooden bed with crisp linens and a vibrant Mexican blanket serves as the room’s centerpiece. Vintage accents—such as a rotary phone, classic books, and rustic décor—complete the space, making the Villa Chamber a cozy, history-rich hideaway perfect for unwinding.

The Villa Chamber – Simple, timeless sophistication.

Bedroom

A warm, rustic bedroom inspired by the Mexican Revolution, featuring a carved wooden bed with colorful Mexican textiles. Portraits of Pancho Villa and Emiliano Zapata hang on the walls alongside vintage décor, with soft amber lighting creating a cozy, immersive atmosphere.

Bathroom

A rustic bathroom with ochre-toned walls and warm lighting, decorated with Mexican Revolution–themed posters and framed portraits. Handmade ceramic fixtures and wooden accents give the space a historic, authentic feel.

Study

A cozy study with dark wood furniture, earth-tone walls, and vintage revolutionary décor. Portraits of Villa and Zapata sit above the desk area, accompanied by antique elements like a rotary phone and old books, giving the room a scholarly, historic character.
