Fiestas Revolucionarias

Celebrate the Spirit of Mexico’s Revolution!

Every weekend, guests are invited to immerse themselves in the vibrant traditions of the Mexican Revolution. Enjoy live corrido music, storytelling of heroic battles, and authentic cuisine inspired by the soldaderas who cooked for the troops.

  • Evening performances of revolutionary folk songs
  • Traditional dishes like tamales and pozole served in the courtyard
  • Guided storytelling sessions about Zapata, Villa, and the soldaderas
  • Exhibits of period photographs and artifacts

Join us for a weekend of courage, culture, and camaraderie — honoring the heroes who shaped Mexico’s destiny.
