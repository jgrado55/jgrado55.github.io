Welcome to La Casa de la Revolución

A Historic Bed & Breakfast Celebrating Mexico’s Revolutionary Spirit

Step back in time at La Casa de la Revolución, a beautifully restored 1910 adobe hacienda located in the heart of historic Mexico. Each of our uniquely themed guest rooms honors a different hero or pivotal moment of the Mexican Revolution (1910–1920) – from Emiliano Zapata and Pancho Villa to the brave soldaderas who fought alongside them.

Wake up to the aroma of fresh coffee and traditional Mexican breakfast served in our sunlit courtyard. Relax among original revolutionary-era photographs, handcrafted talavera tiles, and period furniture while enjoying modern comforts like free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and luxurious linens.

Why Guests Love Staying With Us

  • Authentic historic ambiance with modern amenities
  • Delicious homemade Mexican breakfast included
  • Themed rooms dedicated to revolutionary heroes
  • Walking distance to museums, markets, and cultural sites
  • Warm, personal hospitality from owners who love sharing Mexico’s history
